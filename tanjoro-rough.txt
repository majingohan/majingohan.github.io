Tanjoro- The Sun-Breather

Tanjoro from Demon Slayer starts with Water Breathing, but discovers that we can use the first breathing tecknique, Sun-Breathing.
Pasted down form his father, the sun-breathing takes to form of the Hinokami Kagura.

What other breathing techniques will Tanjoro be able to utilize?  We will see where his training and adventure takes him.
